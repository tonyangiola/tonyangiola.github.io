What’s a SNIPPY?

By Tony Angiola and Katie Paige • October 23, 2025

“What is a Snippy?” A surprising question from a well-known figure in the UFO/UAP world rekindled the San Luis Valley’s most pivotal case — Snippy the Horse.

The Valley of Shadows

The valley’s reputation exploded with the Snippy case, the first animal mutilation to gain worldwide attention, amid reports of luminous craft around Mount Blanca and beyond.

The Horses

In 1967, Snippy was a 3-year-old Appaloosa gelding kept between the Lewis cabin and the King Ranch, alongside Smokey, Sheiba, and Lady.

Discovery & Forensics

Snippy’s remains showed a cauterized collar-like incision, dried vertebrae and skull, and no blood. Forensics reported organ removal without blood and other anomalies.

Environmental Anomalies

Investigators documented a green ooze, unusual ground impressions, and posts seemingly cut by extreme heat — suggesting a massive object had been present.
